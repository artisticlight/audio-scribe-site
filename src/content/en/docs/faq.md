# Frequently Asked Questions

## Does Audio Scribe work offline?

Yes. The on-device AI model works entirely without an internet connection. Your audio is processed locally on your device.

## What devices are supported?

Audio Scribe runs on Mac, iPad, and iPhone. It requires macOS 14+, iPadOS 17+, or iOS 17+.

## Is my data private?

Absolutely. When using the on-device model, no audio or text data ever leaves your device. Cloud models use end-to-end encryption.

## How accurate is the transcription?

Accuracy depends on audio quality and the model selected. The on-device model handles everyday conversations well. Cloud models offer higher accuracy for specialized vocabulary.

## Can I edit transcriptions?

Yes. Audio Scribe includes a full text editor with playback synchronization, so you can tap any word to jump to that moment in the audio.

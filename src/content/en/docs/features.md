# Features

## On-Device AI Processing

Transcribe entirely on your device using Apple's Neural Engine. No internet connection required, and your audio never leaves your device.

## iCloud Sync

All your transcriptions sync seamlessly across Mac, iPad, and iPhone via iCloud. Start on one device, continue on another.

## Cloud Model Support

Opt in to cloud-based AI models for enhanced accuracy with specialized terminology, technical jargon, or multiple languages.

## Export Options

Export your transcriptions as plain text, rich text, SRT subtitles, or share directly with other apps.

## Real-Time Transcription

See your words appear as you speak, with speaker identification and automatic punctuation.

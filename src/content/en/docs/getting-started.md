# Getting Started

Welcome to Audio Scribe. This guide will help you get set up quickly.

## Installation

Download Audio Scribe from the App Store on your Mac, iPad, or iPhone.

## Choosing a Model

Audio Scribe supports both on-device and cloud-based transcription models:

- **On-Device**: Works offline, completely private. Best for everyday use.
- **Cloud Models**: Higher accuracy for specialized vocabulary. Requires internet.

You can switch between models at any time in Settings.
